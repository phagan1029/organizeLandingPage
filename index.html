Features

Organize is desktop software that a company will run on its own computers. It’s built using Microsoft’s state-of-the-art .NET Framework and runs off their industry-standard SQL Server database (the worldwide standard for relational databases). It’s specifically designed to work in a Microsoft Active Directory Domain, which is the industry standard for managing large numbers of computers and users in an enterprise. It can easily be customized to integrate with existing software tools and pull data directly from the systems a company already has in place.

Physical Org Boards – Obsolete

With this fully digital organizing board, there’s no need to figure out how to construct or print something large enough to clearly display. No huge printing presses, no tiny fabricated pieces, all you need is one large screen and your organizing board is there for everyone to see. With Organize's divisional and departmental views, you can also take individual parts of your company and display them in multiple locations, without needing to worry about keeping them all in sync.

Easily Bring About Order

Piece by piece, Organize let’s you lay out your organization using standard Hubbard administrative technology. As you go through your company and take the simple action of filling in your organizing board, you create a real representation of your organization. You don’t have to worry about the physical structure of the organizing board, Organize takes care of all of that for you, all you need to think about is how your company functions. However complex your organization is, Organize gives you the tools to organize it.

Simple yet Robust

Fill in the key information for each division, department section and post; customize the colors and text to fit your company; subdivide divisions; establish your management structure; delineate liaisons. Organize can handle it all. And as you’re designing your organizing board, or as your company grows and changes, you can easily move pieces around to match how your organization actually operates, simply by dragging and dropping sections or posts into their proper place

Manages Staff and Statistics

Easily define an employee’s responsibilities by simply dragging their name onto the appropriate places on your organizing board. Statistics are automatically assigned to him or her based on the posts they are given. When personnel change, simply remove the old employee from the post and add the new one in their place. All the statistics immediately transfer over, and Organize's hat write-up feature lets you easily find the needed material for the newcomer to study in order to properly do their job.

Empower Your Staff

Organize enables each of your employees to report, graph and track their own personal production, giving them a real picture of how they are doing and what areas of their job need attention. Employees are free to create and manage as many statistics as they need in order to organize their job effectively. And all statistics are available to be reviewed by management and can even be made available to the entire company, if so desired, to encourage cooperation and teamwork.

Statistics

Easily create and track any kind of statistic you can dream up. Assign them to posts and group them for efficient management. Set quotas for the day, or for the entire year and let Organize break your target down to daily figures. Automatically calculate new stats based on existing ones using mathematical formulas. Overlay two stats to compare them week to week or over years. Print them out or put them up for display on a monitor. Organize can do all of it, and more.

Consistent Graphs

The layout and spacing of the graphs in Organize are created using measurements taken directly from Hubbard’s administrative materials, and are specifically designed to maintain the same angle, or slope, between data points, regardless of the size of the medium on which they are displayed. Whether printed on a small sheet of paper or blown up on a huge projector, you will be able to reliably and consistently observe the same exact trends. No more wondering about which size graph is showing you the right Condition.

Custom Integration

Having been built on the industry standard .NET Framework and SQL Server database, Organize is ripe with potential for custom integration with existing software. Through these custom additions, you could potentially calculate dozens of stats by pulling data directly from an existing database, CRM or similar system- saving hours that would be wasted on manual entry. We would be happy to examine your systems and provide you with a quote.

Interactive Help

Organize boasts an interactive help feature that can walk unfamiliar users through various procedures from inside the program itself. Rather than having to follow a manual, Organize shows you exactly where to click and where to type, guiding you through each step, so you can learn how it’s done by actually doing it.
